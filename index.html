Privacy Notice

NeepMeat · Trifecta

Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026. This notice explains how the site you currently are on collects, uses, and protects your data.

What Data We Collect

When you create an account on this site, we store your username, a bcrypt-hashed password (we never store your password in plain text), an optional recovery email address, your role, and an optional profile picture you choose to upload.

When you submit a ticket or reply, we store the content you write, any files you attach, the timestamp, and your username as the author.

Data From Discord

This platform is connected to Discord forum channels. When posts or replies are made in the connected Discord server, the following data is automatically imported:

  • Display name - your server nickname or global Discord display name at the time of posting.
  • Profile picture URL - a link to your Discord avatar hosted on Discord's CDN (cdn.discordapp.com). We do not download or store a copy of your avatar; only the URL is kept.
  • Message content - the text of your forum posts and replies.
  • File attachments - any files attached to your Discord messages are downloaded and stored on this server, compressed with Zstandard (zstd) compression.
  • Message ID - Discord's internal snowflake ID, used only to prevent duplicate imports.

This data is imported by an automated bot and is not shared with any third party. No Discord account credentials or tokens belonging to users are ever stored.

How Your Data Is Kept Secure

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. The original password is never saved anywhere.
  • Sessions are secured with a server-side token. Administrators can invalidate all active sessions for any account at any time.
  • Uploaded files are stored compressed (Zstandard level 9) and served with strict Content-Security-Policy headers that prevent any uploaded content from executing scripts.
  • File type enforcement - standard users may only attach images and videos. Contributors and administrators may attach any file type.
  • All databases (users, tickets, tags) are stored as local SQLite files on the server and are not exposed to the network.

Guest Access

Visitors who have not signed in may browse tickets and read replies in a read-only guest mode. Guests cannot view file attachments, post replies, create tickets, or close tickets. No personal data is collected from guest sessions.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists or as long as the associated ticket content is relevant to the server's operations. If you would like your account or data removed, please contact a server administrator directly.

Questions about this privacy notice? Contact a server administrator.
